# Lord Nexus — 100 Mental Models Knowledge Cartridge

Camelot-OS | Reasoning Lens Library

Lord Nexus is the synthesis layer for broad reasoning frameworks. This cartridge organizes 100 mental models into a structured reasoning library that can be mounted by Merlin/Videneptus during planning, strategy, audits, and decision support.

## Purpose

```text
Lord Nexus
= Truth through Pattern
```

The model library prevents single-lens thinking by forcing the system to reason across multiple domains:

- current applied heuristics,
- classical academic frameworks,
- strict scientific validity,
- systems-level synthesis.

## Four-Tentacle Research Pattern

### Velocity

Role: recency and applied knowledge.

Strength:

- Finds current popular models used by modern builders, operators, and strategists.

Risk:

- Can overvalue trendiness and motivational shorthand.

### Archivist

Role: classical grounding.

Strength:

- Preserves durable models from economics, psychology, decision theory, mathematics, physics, biology, and engineering.

Risk:

- Can underweight new applied patterns.

### Skeptic

Role: scientific and logical filter.

Strength:

- Rejects pseudo-models, buzzwords, and catchy phrases that do not support prediction or reasoning.

Risk:

- Can be too narrow for creative strategy.

### Weaver

Role: systems synthesis.

Strength:

- Maps models into connected meta-spheres and reveals interactions.

Risk:

- Can drift into abstraction without operational tests.

## Nexus Synthesis Rule

A mental model is accepted when it satisfies at least one of the following:

1. Predicts behavior under conditions.
2. Explains causality or constraints.
3. Improves decisions under uncertainty.
4. Exposes hidden incentives, feedback, or tradeoffs.
5. Helps decompose or recombine systems.

Reject models that are only slogans.
